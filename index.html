    PANIK KUR!

    The fictional film tells the story of a young man named Kurniawan, who has no other activities besides being active on OMES TV. He hopes to meet someone who shares the same interests as him, specifically a fan of anime and Japanese culture. Kurniawan finally finds what he is looking for, a girl named Fiony. When Kurniawan starts planning to meet Fiony, he becomes nervous and panicked for one reason: his messy hair. Kurniawan tries to find a barber, even though his money is limited. He finds Pak Wahyudi, a street-side barber offering his services. Kurniawan's restless mind causes chaos during his haircut with Pak Wahyudi.

    Bangreng

    Bangreng merupakan kesenian tradisional khas tanah Pasundan yang merupakan transformasi dari kesenian Terbang dan Ronggeng, dua kesenian khas masyarakat Jawa Barat khususnya di kawasan Priangan seperti Sumedang dan Garut. Film dokumenter “Bangreng” mengupas secara mendalam terjadinya evolusi kesenian tersebut yang pada awalnya merupakan kesenian yang kental dengan pesan dakwah ajaran Islam. Film tersebut menuturkan terjadinya proses perubahan yang memunculkan persepsi pro dan kontra di kalangan masyarakat, yang dikemukakan para narasumber dalam film tersebut.
